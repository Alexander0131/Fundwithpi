Privacy Policy for All


Welcome to FundWithPi, a decentralized application (DApp) developed as part of the Pi Network ecosystem, designed to facilitate crowdfunding transactions using Pi Coin.

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our app. By accessing or using FundWithPi, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

a. Personal Information

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we may collect your Pi Network username, wallet address, and other identifiers linked to your Pi Network profile.
  • Transaction Data: Details of your crowdfunding transactions, including the amount of Pi Coin sent or received, project details, and timestamps.
  • Optional Profile Data: If you choose to provide it, your name, email address, or other contact information for communication or campaign purposes.

b. Non-Personal Information

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, such as features used, time spent, and technical data (e.g., device type, IP address, browser type).
  • Analytics: Aggregated data collected to analyze app performance and user trends, which does not personally identify you.

c. Information from Third Parties

We may receive data from the Pi Network blockchain or other integrated DApps to verify transactions or ensure the integrity of your account.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To facilitate crowdfunding transactions using Pi Coin.
  • To verify your identity and ensure the security of your account and transactions.
  • To communicate with you about your campaigns, updates, or support requests.
  • To improve and optimize the app's functionality and user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations or protect against fraud and abuse.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you authorize us to share specific data (e.g., campaign details with donors).
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers (e.g., blockchain nodes, analytics tools) who assist us in operating the app, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of FundWithPi, its users, or the public.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no internet or blockchain-based system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Pi Coin wallet and transaction data are secured by the Pi Network blockchain, and you are responsible for safeguarding your private keys and passwords.
